Research

There are some websites I used as a reference to design and make my website.

  • Upshot Media



    I saw the Upshot media webpage and decided to make a home page like theirs. A background image and then a text in the middle. The background image is added through CSS and is position is set to center with 100% width and height. There were many div tags used in the website to align the text in the middle and there was a link just down below. For me, I've done the same with the background and a text in the middle including the link itself.

  • Marcus Artis


    For the contacts page, I used the the page of Marcus Artis as a reference. The contact form is in the right side of the page in Marcus Artis's page while mine is on the left side. He has a navigation panel on the left side. Since I have a navigation bar at the top, I've added my social media links on the right side of the contacts page.

  • Assemble



    For the blog page, I've taken reference from Assemble website. In their website they've used an image and headers and footers. A heading then a paragraph is written accordingly. I used a similar design for my blog. An introduction first then a picture is added. Below the picture, advantages and disadvantages are parted left and right. On the footer, conclusion is written.

  • Scott Santens


    For the CV part, I've taken Scott Santens website as a reference. He has separated his CV into two parts: left and right and used heading tags and unordered lists with no style to write the information for his CV. For my page, I've done a similar thing. Parting the information left and right. Personal information about me on the left and other information on the right.

  • Teju Cole


    Finally, for the navigationbar, I've used Teju Cole webpage as a reference. He has made a simple navaigation bar. An unordered list is used for the navigation bar. The navigation bar is made inside a border box. For my navigation bar, it is aligned to the center and background is made transparent so the background image is visible.
