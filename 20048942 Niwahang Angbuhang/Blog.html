How Technology has affected our Daily Life

24th May 2021

Technology has been evolving. From the past decades, we've seen a surge of developments in the field of technology. There have been many advancements made in technology. Everyone can access most of their things through their smartphones nowadays. We have been so immersed in technology that we are being dependent on it. Since I am studying computing, I use technology most of my days. As most things have both advantages and disadvantages, using too much technology has its advantages and disadvantages.



Advantages

Technology has helped the world reach states we never thought would be possible. I remember I used to go to my friend’s house just to call him to go play somewhere or to talk to him. Nowadays, I can just phone call or even video call him to chat with my friends, make plans, and play games. I can watch movies and videos in my house without going to the cinemas. We have a small world inside our pockets. Social media helps us to socialize with people from all around the world. I have made many friends from other countries with the help of social media. Also, during these harsh times of COVID, everyone has to stay at home to be safe. The schools and work of most people are made online. My college is currently operating online. If I want to learn some others courses many websites can teach us many new things. Some even teach us for free.

Disadvantages

Compared to other things I believe that technology has fewer disadvantages than advantages. Some advanced technology has made people very lazy. We’ve become lazy to do some simple tasks and hope for scientists or inventors to make it automated. I get on social media most of the time. Most of my days are wasted on social media. I find myself constantly looking at my phone checking Facebook or Instagram. I used to go out a lot before but nowadays I just want to stay inside my house and play games or watch videos. I believe I’ve been somewhat dependent on technology. But being too dependent and being addicted to technology is a bad thing.

One can also say technology is the best thing. If we can get internet connections and network to the rural areas, they won’t have to walk 2-3 hours to get to school. They can learn at home while the education is given properly. Newer technologies with more advanced options are made almost day by day. Compared to the past years and decades, technology nowadays has made our life very easier. The works that needed hours to do can be done in some minutes. There is much potential for technology in the future.
